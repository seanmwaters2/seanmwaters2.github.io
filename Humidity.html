Looking at this graph you may think, "hmm it looks like there's almost no correlation between Latitude and Humidity." You would be right. In a similar study using the same methods, but collecting data from a different date, we found that there was a 0.148 r-value for the nothern hemiphere and a .0257 r-value for the southern hemiphere.
